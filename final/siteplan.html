AstroDaily

"AstroDaily" will be the site name because it will use the APOD API of NASA to show the picture of the day

Purpose

The site will provide a basic splash of astronomy to everyone who is interested in the subject, showcasing a picture-of-the-day feature and also information about telescopes and basic astronomy knowledge.
